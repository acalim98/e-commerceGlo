Glokixs




GloKixs debuted its first store over 5 years ago when Founder & Ceo, Angelo Calim, noticed an increasing demand for popular footwear in the Bay Area. With a vision and a natural sense of entrepreneurship, Bay Area-Born Angelo opened the first Glokixs store on Sonoma Boulevard in Vallejo named after his nickname and slang inspired by the culture.

Currently, Glokixs and its subsidiaries operate over 50 stores across the country's busiest thoroughfares and malls, including more than 10 in the Bay Area. Leading lifestyle, streetwear, and footwear brands are available at the stores, including, but not limited to, Nike, Jordan, Adidas, Polo Footwear, Levi's, and Carhartt. Every day, we receive clothes, shoes, and accessories from fashion designers who are coming up with the hottest looks and hues, giving us a chance to present the most recent trends in clothing for adults, teenagers, and children.
